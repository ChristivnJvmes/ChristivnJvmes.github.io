Issue #042 • Soulslikes, Again

Published September 2025

The Soulslike genre refuses to die — and we’re not complaining. In this issue, we explore why developers keep returning to this formula, what innovations still lie ahead, and how players continue to find fresh challenges in familiar mechanics.

The enduring appeal

Soulslikes thrive on tight combat, cryptic storytelling, and the thrill of overcoming impossible odds. But what keeps the formula interesting is how each new game twists the design — sometimes by introducing speed, verticality, or unique parry systems.

Player-driven discovery

From lore videos to challenge runs, the community has built a culture around discovery. This keeps older titles alive years after release, and encourages developers to leave mysteries unresolved.

What’s next

As more indies tackle the genre, we’re starting to see Soulslikes blend with roguelikes, narrative games, and even cozy aesthetics. The question isn’t whether Soulslikes will continue — it’s how far the definition will stretch.
